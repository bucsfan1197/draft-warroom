Changing scoring instantly re-projects every player and recomputes VORP, replacement levels, and pick recommendations.

Replacement levels (VORP baselines)

VORP = a player's projected points minus the projected points of the last startable player at that position across your league. It's the true draft-day value: a 300-pt RB in a shallow position is worth more than a 320-pt WR in a deep one.
